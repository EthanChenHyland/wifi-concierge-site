Bridging the Digital Divide,

One Client at a Time...


Wifi Concierge is a social entrepreneurship nonprofit initiative dedicated to bridging the Digital Divide among seniors and underserved communities. Our services enable clients to experience an improved connection to the digital world, which will lead to better opportunities and a better quality of life.

Inspiration

A family friend, Mr. Steve, was stuck using an outdated desktop computer, a wired internet connection, and an expensive family phone plan after his wife untimely passed away from cancer.


Wifi Concierge provided him a modern laptop computer, an Android smartphone under an affordable phone plan, and a wireless router which provides him with greater mobility and an improved internet connection.

Testimonials

At Wifi Concierge, we offer quality customer service one can depend on for improved connectivity. We assist seniors and less fortunate communities with professional services. It is our objective to help narrow the digital divide by offering customized services to our clients and thereby increasing their confidence in today's rather challenging digital society. Our patience, empathy, and compassion makes technology simple and easily accessible for everyone.
